The Real Mojito

Behold, The Legitimate Mojito

If you're not a fan of alochol you'll likely enjoy this, not as dry as other cocktails, it's wet...hence Mojito

Rumor has it that if you drink enough of this majestic drink, you'll start rambling in spanish

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2meidum lime, cut into 3 wedges, divided
  • 2 tablesponns white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 cup ice cubes, or as needed
  • 11/2fluid ounces white rum
  • 1/2cup club soda, or as needed

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass. Use a muddler and crush to release mint oils and lime juice.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add remaining lime wedges and 2 tablespoons sugar, and muddle again to release the lime juice. Do not strain the mixture.

  3. Step 3

    4. Fill the glass almost to the top with ice. Pour in rum and fill the glass with club soda

  4. Step 4

    5. Stir, taste, and add more sugar if desired.
