Microwave Chocolate Mug Cake

Behold, A Chocolate Mug Cake

This recipe requires chocolate enthusiasm, let your creativity wander and put that deliciousness in a cup, don't drink it...sip

Interesting thing about chocolate cake recipes is that I don't care about any other ingredient but chocolate, so I have never viewed any iteration of chocolate as different, chocolate is perfect in any form.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/8Teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/8teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1/4teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a large microwave-safe mug; stir in milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract.

  2. Step 2

    3. Cook in microwave until cake is done in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.
